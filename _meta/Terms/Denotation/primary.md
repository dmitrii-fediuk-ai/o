#
In philosophy and linguistics, the denotation of **a word or expression is its strictly literal meaning**

#
Denotation plays a major role in several fields. 
Within semantics and philosophy of language, denotation is studied as an important aspect of meaning. 
In mathematics and computer science, assignments of denotations are assigned to expressions are a crucial step in defining interpreted formal languages.
The main task of formal semantics is to reverse engineer the computational system which assigns denotations to expressions of natural languages.
